Our Story

Built on Quality.
Rooted in the Ozarks.

Shipp-Mates Epoxy of the Ozarks was founded with one mission: bring professional-grade floor coatings to the Lake of the Ozarks homeowners and businesses at a fair price, with honest work and zero shortcuts.

We're not a national franchise. We're not a fly-by-night operation. We're a local, owner-operated company based in Osage Beach, Missouri — and every single job we take on, we treat like it's our own home.

We've watched too many homeowners get burned by cheap epoxy jobs that peel within a year. Floors installed by companies who skip the prep, use inferior materials, and disappear when problems arise. We built Shipp-Mates Epoxy of the Ozarks specifically to be the alternative to that — a company you can actually trust.

Why We're Different

Every install starts with full diamond grinding — the step most cheap installers skip. We use professional-grade polyaspartic/polyurea hybrid coatings, not the water-based epoxy from a box. And every single project is backed by our written lifetime warranty, because we stand behind our work long after the truck leaves your driveway.

We serve a 75-mile radius around Osage Beach — from Laurie to Lebanon, Versailles to Tuscumbia — and we bring the same level of care to every job regardless of size.

    Diamond Grinding Standard
