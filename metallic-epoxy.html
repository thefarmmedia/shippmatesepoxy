Every Metallic Floor Is a One-of-a-Kind Work of Art

Metallic epoxy floors are the most visually stunning option we install. Every single pour creates a completely unique three-dimensional pattern — liquid metal frozen in time, with depth and movement that no other flooring system can replicate.

Perfect for luxury garages, showrooms, high-end basements, mancaves, and commercial spaces where first impressions matter. Our metallic system is applied over our standard polyaspartic base for maximum durability — this isn't just beautiful, it's built to last.

No Two Metallic Floors Are Ever the SameThe swirling, blending process creates a truly unique piece of art on your floor. You'll never see the same floor twice — that's the magic of metallic epoxy.
