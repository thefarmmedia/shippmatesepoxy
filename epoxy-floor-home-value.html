Does an Epoxy Garage Floor Increase Your Home's Value?

Shipp-Mates Epoxy of the Ozarks · December 2024 · 5 min read

The Short Answer: Yes — and It's One of the Best ROI Upgrades You Can Make

A professionally installed garage floor coating consistently ranks among the highest ROI home improvement projects you can complete. Here's why real estate professionals talk about it the way they do.

What Real Estate Agents Say

Real estate agents who work in the Lake of the Ozarks will tell you: a finished garage floor makes a home show significantly better. Buyers walk into a garage with a beautiful coated floor and it reads as "well-maintained home." Walk into a garage with cracked, oil-stained bare concrete and it reads as "unknown maintenance history."

The psychology matters. Buyers form an overall impression of a home based on what they see, and a professional garage floor signals that the current owner cared about details throughout the property.

The Numbers

While exact ROI varies by market, here's the typical picture for a the Lake of the Ozarks home:

  • Cost of professional install: Use our Instant Price Calculator to get a ballpark for your specific project
  • Perceived value increase to buyers: Varies by market — real estate agents consistently report coated garage floors make homes show significantly better and sell faster
  • ROI: Often exceeds the install cost for the right buyer in the right market

Beyond the direct financial return, a coated floor can be the difference between a buyer choosing your home over a comparable one — or the reason your home sells faster than others on the market.

The Durability Argument

A professionally installed polyaspartic floor that's 2 years old is worth more to a buyer than a DIY coat that's peeling at the edges. The quality of the install matters enormously. Buyers (and their agents) can tell the difference.

If you're installing a floor coating to help sell your home, invest in a professional system with a warranty. A lifetime warranty is a transferable asset you can hand to the next owner — and that's a selling point you can actually put in the listing.

Beyond Resale: The Daily Value

Even if you never sell, a quality garage floor delivers real daily value. Your garage becomes a usable room. It's easier to clean. You can actually use the space for hobbies, fitness, or storage without embarrassment. For most homeowners, this daily quality-of-life improvement is worth the investment regardless of resale.
