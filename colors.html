What Makes Hybrid Flake Different?

Hybrid flake blends multiple chip sizes — typically ¼" standard flake mixed with smaller and larger sizes — creating a richer, more layered visual with greater depth and dimension. The result is a more premium, high-end look. Available for a small upcharge over our standard ¼" system. Ask us at your free quote appointment and we'll show you samples of both side by side.

What You're Getting

A 4-Layer Professional
Coating System

Every floor we install is a complete 4-layer system — not just a coat of paint rolled on top. This is what separates a floor that lasts 15+ years from one that peels in 18 months.

1
Diamond Ground Concrete

Mechanically abraded to create the profile needed for permanent adhesion. No acid etching. Ever.

2
Polyaspartic Primer Coat

Penetrates deep into the concrete, locks out moisture, and creates the bond layer for the full system.

3
Full Broadcast Flake Layer

Your chosen color blend is broadcast into the wet base coat at 100% coverage — corner to corner, no bare spots.

4
UV-Stable Polyaspartic Topcoat

The protective clear layer. Scratch-resistant, chemical-resistant, UV-stable. This is what the lifetime warranty covers.
