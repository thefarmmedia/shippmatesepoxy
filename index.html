How It Works

Every install follows the same meticulous process. No shortcuts. No skipped steps. That's why our floors last 15+ years.

1
Surface Prep

Diamond grinding removes all coatings, oils, and imperfections. Cracks filled and leveled. This step is everything.

UV-stable, chemical-resistant topcoat locks it all in. The result: a floor that's gorgeous and maintenance-free for 15 years.

How long does an epoxy garage floor last?▾
With professional-grade polyaspartic coatings and full diamond grinding prep, expect 15–25 years of durability. Our system is backed by a written lifetime warranty against fading, peeling, and yellowing.
Is polyaspartic better than regular epoxy?▾
Significantly. Polyaspartic/polyurea hybrid coatings are up to 6× stronger than standard epoxy, UV stable (won't yellow in sunlight), more flexible (won't crack with temperature swings), and cure far faster. It's the professional-grade option we use on every install.
How long before I can drive on my new floor?▾
Because we use polyaspartic coatings, your floor is typically ready for foot traffic within hours and vehicle traffic within 24 hours. Most jobs are done in a single day start to finish.
Why do some epoxy floors peel and fail?▾
The #1 cause of floor failure is skipping or cutting corners on surface preparation. Most cheap installers skip diamond grinding or use acid etching, which doesn't create enough profile for proper adhesion. We use full diamond grinding on every job — no exceptions, ever.
How much does an epoxy garage floor cost in Missouri?▾
Pricing depends on square footage, floor condition, and system chosen. A typical 2-car garage (400–600 sq ft) ranges from $1,800–$3,500. We provide free quotes with honest pricing and no surprises. Use our form above to get your estimate.
Do you repair cracks and damage before coating?▾
Yes. All cracks and surface damage are filled and leveled as part of our standard prep process before any coating is applied. We fix problems — we don't coat over them.
Can you install coatings in the winter?▾
Yes, with proper conditions. We install year-round in most situations. Concrete surface temperature must meet a minimum threshold. We evaluate conditions on every project and advise accordingly.
What flake colors and coating systems do you offer?▾
We carry dozens of flake blends including Tidal Wave, Orbit, Outback, Nightfall, Domino, Gravel, and many more. We also offer metallic epoxy systems, solid colors, and commercial broadcast systems. Visit our Colors page to see all options.
Do you serve areas outside Osage Beach?▾
Absolutely. We serve a full 75-mile radius around Osage Beach, MO — including Camdenton, Lake Ozark, Versailles, Eldon, Laurie, Linn Creek, Stover, Tuscumbia, Lebanon, and many more communities throughout the Lake of the Ozarks.
